Hi, I am Andrea,

UX Designer | Cat Mom | Plant Enthusiast

I am an inquisitive, imaginative and adaptive UX Designer living in the DMV area.

I am passionate about solving problems, brainstorming creative ideas and implementing them in the real world. My skills include user centric research, information architecture, and visual prototyping on tools like Adobe XD, Figma, InVision and Sketch.

When I’m not learning something new, taking part in a Adobe XD Challenge, or taking care of my plants, you can find me curled up with a historic fiction book and my cats Obi Wan and Vader.

My Journey to UX

I found my way to UX design from a successful career in formal education and marketing which gives me a strong background in human psychology, empathy and branding.

Visualizing information and making it “eye catching” became my thing when I started my first job as a teacher. Getting student’s attention for 45 minutes or longer was a challenge I succeeded by creating student-centred lessons and understanding the way humans learn and behave.

As an associate marketing assistant I expanded my skillset to include: drafting potential client engagement strategies, creating campaigns, drafting promotion material, and branding.

Now, in 2020 I am ready to take on the challenge of applying the skills I’ve learned in my professional career and from the UX UI bootcamp at GWU and make UX design my new full-time career.

What I can do for you

User Research

I specialize in conducting effective user research that includes industry benchmarking, user interviews, surveys, user testing and data analysis.

I also carry out competitive analysis in order to have a solid idea on where a product stands in the industry and where to take it.

Visual Prototyping

Through tools like Adobe XD, Figma, InVision and Sketch I am able to portray ideas and solutions based on user’s needs, pain points and frustrations.

I also enjoy taking part in design challenges by the Adobe XD community to keep my skills sharp and be informed on the latest features.

Front End Development

I am able to bring my design vision to life using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, and browser based javascript.
